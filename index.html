Our ambition

We believe that financially rewarding players for their time and effort is the key driver for future growth of the video game market and its global adoption, especially in relation to eSports.

And if you are a young person with eSport ambitions, you should not have to wait months before you can participate in exhausting process of tournament qualifiers with large prize money. If unsuccessful, then your time and effort should not go unnoticed.

We perceive it as a job, we treat the entry fees as a fee you'd have to pay to get to work and we pay you for your time.
